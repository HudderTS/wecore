FAQ

Picking the right product

Quick answers for choosing between WECORE products

Cloud Servers are individual virtual machines you manage yourself — ideal for web apps, dev/test, and single services. Cloud HPC is a managed Slurm cluster for batch and parallel scientific workloads, where a scheduler queues jobs across many nodes. Reach for HPC when you submit jobs; reach for Cloud Servers when you want a machine.

Private Cloud (OpenStack on dedicated bare metal) gives you full VMs and a single-tenant cloud you control end to end — best for VMware migration, compliance, and mixed workloads. Kubernetes is the right fit when your applications are already containerized and you want a managed control plane to orchestrate them. Many teams run Kubernetes on top of Private Cloud.

QuFabric, our quantum simulation platform, is coming soon — it's built from the same WECORE HPC, Kubernetes, and Cloud Server primitives. You can request early access from the QuFabric page and we'll bring you in as capacity opens.

Yes. Every product can run in WECORE's cloud, private on your own infrastructure, or hybrid — joining your hardware to the platform and bursting into the cloud when demand spikes. The Cloud Panel workflow is the same across all three.

Absolutely — that's the point of one platform. Run a Private Cloud, schedule HPC jobs against it, orchestrate services with Kubernetes, and spin up Cloud Servers for everything in between — all from a single account, wallet, and panel.
