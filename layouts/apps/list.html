Blipify is a dynamic integration that connects your product ecosystem with customer feedback channels, allowing teams to receive, track, and act on insights as they happen. Whether it's from surveys, reviews, support chats, or in-app feedback, Blipify ensures no signal is missed—so you can move faster and make smarter product decisions.

Key Features

  • Real-Time Feedback Sync — Instantly pull customer comments, ratings, and suggestions into your preferred tools
  • Feedback Prioritization Engine — Uses sentiment analysis and usage impact to surface the most critical feedback first
  • Cross-Tool Linking — Connects feedback to specific product features or tickets in systems
  • Custom Alerts & Triggers — Set rules to notify the right teams when key phrases, negative sentiment, or VIP users are detected
  • Smart Tagging & Routing — Monthly insights on KPIs, trends, and workflow efficiency

How It Works

  1. Connect your feedback sources—like Typeform, Intercom, Zendesk, or Canny.
  2. Customize your tagging, alert, and prioritization preferences.
  3. Sync feedback automatically to your team’s favorite platforms.
  4. Act on insights with linked action items and real-time updates.

Pricing

  • Free: Up to 2 feedback sources, basic sync
  • Team ($29/month): 10 sources, smart tagging, team alerts
  • Pro ($99/month): Unlimited sources, advanced routing, sentiment engine, analytics

Blipify helps you move beyond collecting feedback—transforming it into real-time, actionable insights your whole team can use. Start syncing, start aligning, and start building better experiences today.
