Module features

Everything the Cloud Panel HPC module gives your users — identical across cloud, private, and hybrid.
Slurm platform topology
Provision controller, login, and compute roles as one coordinated platform, with SSH entry and scheduler services prepared for users.
Template-driven sizing
Shape controller, login, and compute capacity from cluster templates and HPC-tagged flavors — no hand-built infrastructure.
Purpose-built compute pools
Add specialist GPU, high-memory, or general-purpose pools so each workload lands on the right Slurm partition.
