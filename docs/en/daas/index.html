Trade expensive desktops and refresh cycles for desktops you rent by the month. Turn capital expense into predictable operating cost, and right-size capacity as your team grows or shrinks.

What DaaS is, how it works, and whether it fits your team

DaaS delivers full virtual desktops hosted in WECORE's OpenStack cloud and streamed to whatever device your users already have. We build and run the infrastructure — compute, storage, networking, the connection broker, and the login portal — so you don't build or maintain VDI yourself. Users log in and get a desktop; you manage the images and policies.

A persistent desktop is dedicated to one user and keeps their installed apps, files, and settings between sessions — ideal for knowledge workers. A pooled (non-persistent) desktop is cloned from a golden image at login and returns to the pool on logout — ideal for task workers, labs, and shared kiosks where every session starts clean. You can offer both on the same platform.

Users reach their desktop through a single secure login portal from a laptop, tablet, thin client, or browser. The connection broker picks the right display protocol per desktop — RDP for Windows, VNC and NoMachine NX for Linux — so each workload gets a responsive session, including over modest connections.

Yes. We attach NVIDIA GPUs to desktops — a full GPU for heavy workstations or a vGPU slice shared across several desktops. That covers CAD, 3D and GIS, video editing, simulation, and ML, with no physical workstation to ship or maintain.

Desktops run inside our datacenter and only pixels are streamed to the endpoint — files, code, and credentials never land on the device. Combined with per-tenant network isolation, a zero-trust login portal, and idle-session auto-lock, this keeps data centralized and makes remote work and compliance far easier to control.

DaaS is billed monthly per desktop, with GPU desktops priced as an add-on. Cost control is built in: idle desktops auto-lock and power off, and pooled desktops return to the pool on logout — so you pay for desktops in use, not idle sessions. Scale the pool up for a project and back down when it ends.

Yes. You can start from our maintained Windows and Linux base images or upload your own golden image with your apps and security baseline preinstalled. Update the master once and roll the change out to every pooled desktop on next login.

WECORE manages: the OpenStack platform, hypervisors and GPUs, storage, networking, the connection broker and portal, and managed monitoring. You manage: your desktop images, the apps inside them, users and access policies, and which roles get persistent versus pooled desktops. We run the platform so your team runs the desktops.
